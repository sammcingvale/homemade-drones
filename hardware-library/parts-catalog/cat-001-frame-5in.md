# 5-inch Quadcopter Frame (Source One V5)

**Catalog ID:** cat-001
**Status:** active
**Last verified:** 2026-05-05

## Abstract spec

- 5-inch prop class (≤220mm motor-to-motor diagonal, X-config)
- Open-source design (multiple manufacturers must produce it)
- 30.5×30.5mm FC stack mounting holes (with 20×20mm fallback pattern is fine but not required)
- ≥3mm 3K twill carbon fiber arms
- Replaceable arms (single-arm replacement <$10)
- M3 standard fasteners throughout
- Compatible with 1404–2207 motor mount patterns

## Notes

- Spare arms are essential — Source One V5 arms break in moderate crashes. Carry spares.
- Top plate not strictly required for v1 (we 3D-print our own canopy). Some kits ship without one.
- After two builds, evaluate whether to switch to a stiffer/more expensive frame (ImpulseRC Apex) or move to printed PA-CF structural plate (graduates to v2 design).
