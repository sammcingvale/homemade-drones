## Reorder guidance

~150–200g per drone of PETG used for canopies, masts, battery trays. 1kg = ~5 drones of PETG parts.

Suggested trigger: on hand ≤ 1. Suggested order qty: 1.

## Notes

- Quantity is in **spools**, not grams. Track partial spools as fractional usage in build notes if needed.
- Bambu's RFID tags auto-configure print settings on the P2S — saves slicer fiddling. Worth the slight premium.
- Dry filament before printing if it's been open >2 weeks. Wet PETG = stringy prints + weak layer adhesion.
- PETG creep onset ~75°C — fine for canopies but **not for parts touching motor mounts** (motor housings hit ~60°C at hover with 5°C margin only).
