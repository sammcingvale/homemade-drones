## Notes

- Install with soldering iron at 240°C, dedicated insert-installation tip preferred (or use a smooth conical tip you don't mind getting brass on).
- Press in slowly; let the iron's heat pull the insert in. Forcing it cracks the print.
- Insert should sit 0.1–0.2mm proud of the surface, then is squashed flush by the mating screw — this gives best pull-out strength.
- Design printed parts with 4.2mm holes and short lead-in chamfers (0.5mm × 45°).
