## Notes

- **Leaded solder is easier to learn with.** Lead-free flows worse, requires hotter iron, more skill. Use leaded for v1; switch to lead-free once you're comfortable if you care about RoHS/health.
- Wash hands after soldering. Don't eat at the soldering bench.
- Old/oxidized solder = bad joints. If your spool is >5 years old, replace it.
