## Reorder guidance

Props are the most-broken part on the drone. Keep at least 2 sets on hand (8 props = 1 set per motor × 2 spares). Reorder 4 sets at a time.

Suggested trigger: on hand ≤ 8. Suggested order qty: 16.

## Notes

- Inspect props before every flight. **Any nick, crack, or chip = retire the prop.** Imbalanced props cause motor heat and bad IMU data.
- Cheap; don't be precious. Retire on first sign of damage.
- Buy in bulk. v1 field kit lists 8 spare props as standard.
- Quantity is tracked per **prop**, not per set.
