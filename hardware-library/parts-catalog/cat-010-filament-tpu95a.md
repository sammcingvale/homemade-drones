## Reorder guidance

~50g per drone of TPU for soft mounts and antenna tubes. 1kg = ~20 drones of TPU parts. Reorder when down to last spool.

Suggested trigger: on hand ≤ 1. Suggested order qty: 1.

## Notes

- **Note SKU corrected from BOM v1.0:** Bambu sells TPU 95A HF only in 1kg (not 0.5kg as previously listed).
- Print slow (≤30 mm/s). TPU stringing at high speed is unfixable.
- Minimize retraction — TPU is elastic and retraction creates stringing on small features.
- Store in dry box. Wet TPU prints with bubbles and weakens significantly.
