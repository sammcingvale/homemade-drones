## Notes

- Quantity tracked in **kits**, not individual screws. Don't try to count every M3 screw.
- Apply Loctite 243 (blue) to motor screws and any vibration-loaded threads.
- **Don't mix** stainless and alloy in the same airframe — galvanic corrosion isn't an issue but inconsistent thread feel can mask problems.
